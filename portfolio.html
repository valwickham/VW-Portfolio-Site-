GetInked

Problem Statement and Purpose

Our users need a way to obtain general information related to getting a tattoo, and they also need to be able to explore their options with regards to design and tattoo artists, because our users must feel satisfied with their since tattoos are a life long commitment , which is being displayed on their bodies.

Overall Process

I first found the Potential Solution to the Problem Statement. I then proceeded with Competitive Analysis, by looking at other apps that would offer similar solutions, and tried to find their weaknesses and potential areas for improvements. I then did the Research through Surveys and also Interviews, so that I could get a better understanding of what people were looking for when using a tattoo app. The next step was to create User Personas, who reflected a summary of my findings from surveys and interviews, and I traced User Journeys to better understand the challenges that my users are facing, and I created Flows, to lead my users to be able to solve tasks when using the app. I then worked on improving the Sitemap, to make sure that users would find the information they needed in the most efficient and easiest way. Afterwards, I started working on sketches and wireframing the different screens for my app. The biggest step was getting to prototype the wireframes and conduct Usability Testing, to verify that users were able to understand how to use the app and whether there were any missing passages/steps. I then worked on revising my prototype, by using the results from the testing, and I got to the Final Design which I then published online.
