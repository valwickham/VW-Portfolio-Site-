My Story

I am originally from Milan, Italy, and I have been lucky to have lived in a few different Countries before settling in the United States.

Prior to UX Design, I worked in the Health and Wellness Industry, which gave me the building blocks to understand how to approach the emotional and behavioral processes of people from all walks of life. I worked on projects that relied heavily on empathizing with individuals. As an experienced professional in the tech industry, I understand the design needs that customers have and know how to deliver solutions that are up to customer satisfaction. Making a change into UX Design is one of the best decisions I have made for my career. It allows for me to craft products that engage users in a way that is able to change perspectives and user experiences for the better.
