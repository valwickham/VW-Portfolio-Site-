Hello! My name is Valentina and I am a UX Designer based in the State of California, USA.

I graduated from the University of Texas at San Antonio with a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology/Exercise Science and a Minor in Wellness; therefore my background isn’t related to design, but I have always had a passion for art due to my cultural background. In my previous career as personal trainer and as aid in physical therapy, I put a lot dedication into listening to my clients, to empathize with them, and I have always worked hard to understand my clients’ goals and preferences, so that we could work together to achieve whatever objective they had their mind. I work with passion to make sure that my clients feel constantly supported and taken care of.

I am now looking to find a job placement that will allow me to enter into the rewarding job field of UX Design!
